How it works: This tool randomly generates 10 FontAwesome icons to spark your creativity. Originally inspired by the YouTube video "Easy Ways to be your own GM (solo RPGing)" by Geek Gamers, who suggested using dice with pictures for story inspiration.

Creative tip: If you're running a low-tech fantasy setting but get modern icons (like a motorcycle), think about the period-appropriate equivalent (like a horse or magical steed).
