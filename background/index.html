👤 Character Background Generator

Create rich backstories and backgrounds for NPCs and player characters

Generate unique character backgrounds using card properties from the open source Arcmage card game. This tool randomly combines different family traits and characteristics to create interesting character concepts.

Perfect for:

  • Creating memorable NPCs with depth
  • Inspiring player character backstories
  • Adding variety to your cast of characters
  • Breaking out of typical character archetypes
